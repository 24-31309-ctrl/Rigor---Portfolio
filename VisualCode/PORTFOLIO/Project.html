This project is a filmed adaptation highlighting key scenes and themes from Noli Me Tangere and El Filibusterismo, aiming to bring the stories to life through visual storytelling and modern interpretation. The production was shot in selected local settings that were carefully chosen to reflect the historical atmosphere and social environment depicted in the novels, helping to enhance authenticity and audience engagement.
